This Privacy Policy describes how Gogaku ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Gogaku mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your username, email address, and a securely hashed password. We do not store passwords in plain text.

Learning Activity

We store data related to your use of the App so that your progress persists across sessions and devices. This includes lessons completed, flashcards reviewed, dictionary searches, custom flashcard decks, and AI chat history.

Purchase Information

If you purchase a subscription or lifetime access, your payment is processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card information. We do receive a confirmation that a purchase was made and the type of plan associated with your account.

Device & Usage Information

We may collect basic technical information such as device type, operating system version, and app version for diagnostic and improvement purposes. We do not collect precise location data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App and its features
  • To save and sync your learning progress across devices
  • To process subscriptions and lifetime purchases
  • To improve and debug the App
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

OpenAI

The AI conversation feature is powered by OpenAI. When you send a message through the AI chat, your message and relevant context (such as your current lesson level and recent flashcards) are transmitted to OpenAI to generate a response. OpenAI's use of this data is governed by their own privacy policy. We do not send your account email, password, or other personal identifiers to OpenAI.

Apple

Purchases are handled by Apple via the App Store. Apple's privacy practices apply to any payment information you provide.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with advertisers. We share data with third parties only when:

  • It is necessary to provide a feature you have requested (e.g., sending a chat message to OpenAI)
  • It is required by law or legal process
  • It is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Gogaku, our users, or the public

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including encrypted transmission (HTTPS) and hashed password storage. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, but we work to follow industry best practices.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing privacy@gogaku.app. Some information may be retained for legal, financial, or backup purposes for a limited period after deletion.

7. Children's Privacy

Gogaku is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export the personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, email privacy@gogaku.app.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify users through the App or by email. The "Effective Date" above will reflect the most recent revision.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email privacy@gogaku.app.
